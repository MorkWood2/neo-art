Idea

so here I am, next to the most important city in the world and I'm not even going to explore

All I had was a blueprint in my head. I imagined an interactive Google map documenting a sprawl of public art in the Bay Area. From big commissioned pieces laid out on a 5 story building to a tiny sticker on a post in West Oakland, the image was there, I just had no idea where to start.

Visual representation of my thought process...

Luckily at the time I was a regular at the anarcho hackerspace Noisebridge in the Mission, where many of my mentors, fellow programmers and people with vast industry knowledge coincided, allowing me a perfect opportunity to run my ideas, get advice, and start building. So it begun.
